## Managing Leads

### View All Leads

1. Click **"Leads"** in the menu
2. See all leads assigned to you
3. Filter by status or search by name

### Add a New Lead

1. Click **"+ New Lead"** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Name** - Lead's name
   - **Phone** - Contact number (with country code: +91...)
   - **Email** - Email address
   - **Relation** - Parent, Student, etc.
   - **Class** - Class interested in (KG, Class 1-12)
   - **Source** - How they found us (Website, Referral, Call, etc.)
   - **Budget** - Economy/Standard/Premium
   - **Notes** - Any additional information
3. Click **"Add Lead"**

### Update a Lead

1. Click on a lead card
2. View their full details
3. Click **"Edit Notes"** to update information
4. Click **"Save"**

### Lead Status

- **New** - Just added, not contacted
- **Contacted** - You've called or emailed
- **Interested** - They showed interest
- **Follow-up** - Need to follow up soon
- **Enrolled** - Admission complete ✅

## Logging Calls

### Log a Call After Speaking with Parent

1. Click **"Calls"** in menu
2. Click **"+ Log Call"** button
3. Fill in:
   - **Lead** - Select from dropdown
   - **Date & Time** - When the call happened
   - **Duration** - How many minutes
   - **Outcome** - Positive / Negative / Neutral
   - **Notes** - What was discussed
4. Click **"Save Call"**

### What to Include in Notes

✅ **Good notes:**
- "Parent interested in Class 9. Discussed fees and academics. Will call back next week."
- "Student performing well. Parent wants campus tour. Scheduled for Saturday."
- "Need documents: Marksheet, BC, Residence proof. Will send list via email."

❌ **Bad notes:**
- "Talked to parent"
- "OK"
- "Call done"

## Creating Reminders

### Schedule a Follow-up

1. Click **"Reminders"** in menu
2. Click **"+ New Reminder"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Lead** - Who to follow up with
   - **Type** - What action:
     - Follow-up Call
     - Send Documents
     - Schedule Visit
     - Check Payment
   - **Date** - When to remind
   - **Time** - What time
   - **Description** - Details
4. Click **"Create Reminder"**

### You'll Get Notified When It's Time:
- ✉️ Email reminder
- 📱 SMS (if enabled)
- 🔔 In-app notification

## Common Tasks

### Task: Parent Called About Admission

1. Add new lead with their info
2. Log a call right away
3. Create reminder to send brochure
4. Update lead status to "Contacted"

### Task: Interested Parent Needs Campus Tour

1. Find lead in "Leads" section
2. Update status to "Interested"
3. Log note: "Scheduled campus tour for Saturday 10 AM"
4. Create reminder: "Confirm tour with parent" (Friday afternoon)
5. Create reminder: "Welcome parent at gate" (Saturday morning)

### Task: Parent Sent Documents

1. Open lead details
2. Update notes: "Received all required documents"
3. Change status to "Follow-up"
4. Create reminder: "Follow up on admission status" (2 days)
5. Log call once you speak with them

### Task: Enroll a Student

1. Open lead record
2. Change status to "Enrolled"
3. Add note: "Enrollment completed. Class 9-A"
4. Log final call: "Admission confirmed"

## Tips & Tricks

### ⚡ Quick Tips

1. **Search Faster**
   - Type in search box to filter leads
   - Search by name, phone, or email

2. **Filter by Status**
   - Click filter dropdown
   - See all leads with same status

3. **Mobile Friendly**
   - Can manage everything on phone
   - Add leads while on campus visits

4. **Data Syncs Automatically**
   - Your updates sync to Google Sheets
   - Admin can see everything in real-time

5. **Print Reports**
   - Click "Reports" if you're admin
   - Download as CSV/PDF

### 🎯 Best Practices

1. **Log calls IMMEDIATELY**
   - Don't wait till end of day
   - You'll forget important details

2. **Use clear, detailed notes**
   - Other team members might follow up
   - Admin might review

3. **Create reminders early**
   - Set up follow-ups right after call
   - Don't rely on memory

4. **Update lead status**
   - Helps track conversion
   - Shows progress to management

5. **Be consistent**
   - Use same phone format (+91...)
   - Be consistent with spelling

## FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?
**A:** Click "Forgot Password" on login page or contact your admin.

### Q: Can I see other people's leads?
**A:** No, you only see leads assigned to you. Admins can see all leads.

### Q: How do I reassign a lead to another team member?
**A:** You can't. Contact your admin to reassign.

### Q: Will my leads sync with Google Sheets?
**A:** Yes, automatically every 30 seconds. No manual action needed.

### Q: Can I delete a lead?
**A:** Only admins can delete. Contact them if you need to remove a lead.

### Q: How do I export my data?
**A:** Click "Reports" → "Download as CSV" (admin feature).

### Q: Will I get SMS reminders?
**A:** Yes, if your phone number is added in settings.

### Q: What if I'm not available for a reminder?
**A:** Click "Snooze" to move it to later or "Mark Done" if completed.

### Q: Can I edit a call log?
**A:** Yes, click the call → "Edit" (only shows your own calls).

### Q: What time format should I use?
**A:** Use 24-hour format (9:30 AM = 09:30, 2:15 PM = 14:15).
